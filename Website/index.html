Egg Fun Facts

  • While many people eat eggs for breakfast, 70% of eggs in Europe go into processed foods like pasta, pastries, sauces, and desserts.
  • Eggs contain all 9 essential amino acids — making them one of the most nutrient-dense and efficient protein sources.
  • A laying hen can produce over 300 eggs annually.
